FAQ

Are the ingredients fresh?

     Yes, all the fruits are freshly picked every morning. The sugar and sweeteners are also all natural.


Are the drinks allergy friendly?

     Yes, we don't use nuts or any other foods that are common allergies.


Are the drinks overpriced?

     No, in fact, we have the some of the cheapest prices for a drink of this size in the world.


Can I order a custom juice?

     Yes, just tell the staff what fruits you would like to be in it.


What is the "fan favorite"?

     The favorite is probably cranberry juice.


Can I buy plain fruit?

     Yes, but that it is more expensive to buy fruit and make juice than just buying the juice.
