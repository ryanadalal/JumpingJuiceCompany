Make Your Own Orange Juice

Home Mailing List How To Loactions Contact FAQ

Things you will need

  • Large bowl
  • Small bowl
  • Spoon
  • Low power microwave

Steps

  1. Pour water into a bowl.
  2. Mix in sugar till disolved.
  3. Pour in milk.
  4. Squeze oranges over a small bowl.
  5. Heat both containers in the microwave seperatly on low power for 30 seconds.
  6. Slowly pour the oranges substance into the water milk solution.

Ingredients

  • Water - One Quart
  • Oranges - 10
  • Sugar - 3 tbs
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
